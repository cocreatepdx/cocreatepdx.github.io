FAQ

Are the materials included?

Yes, the materials you need for each workshop are included. You may view the cost of materials on each workshop page.

Do I keep the materials?

You keep all of the materials that are a part of the finished object you make. Depending on the workshop, you may also keep the tool(s) used to create what you make. See each workshop page for full details about what you keep.

I have my ticket. Do I need to do or bring anything before I attend the workshop?

Depending on the workshop, you may need to provide additional information to us (e.g. make a selection) or gather a personal material to bring (e.g. picture of your dog). Just check the page of the workshop you plan to attend and read the “Before The Workshop” section.

Do you do workshops for children and adults?

At this time we only do workshops for adults.

Do I need to have any art or craft making experience to attend?

You do not need any experience; there are no prerequisites.

What if I need to back out - is my ticket refundable?

Workshops are non-refundable, but tickets can be exchanged for the same dollar amount towards other workshops ONLY when you give a one week cancellation notice. In the event of a workshop cancellation, tickets are refundable or exchangeable.
