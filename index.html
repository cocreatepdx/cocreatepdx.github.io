Hello! ☺ We’re Christopher and Tricia - two forever curious, creative guides calling “eclectic experts and delighted dilettantes” alike to join us for art and craft adventures.

Between the two of us we share thirty years of creative life training, which includes a BFA in Graphic Design, BS in Business Administration, Computer Information Systems, BFA in Illustration. We are makers who love to share and cocreate! We can’t wait to grow with you in our seasonal collections of creative workshops.
