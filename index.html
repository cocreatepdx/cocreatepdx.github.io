Neko Diablo


About

Introduction

Neko Diablo offers creative workshops in Portland, Oregon, where like-minded explorers can learn new skills and make memorable art and gifts together.

Do you call yourself an eclectic expert or delighted dilettante? A maker at heart – or maybe a budding artist? Would you like to explore more of your creative side and meet new people in Portland?

Neko Diablo offers a variety of creative workshops where you do just that! Become an explorer and help determine upcoming workshops – share what you want to learn and make.
