Hand-carve a monogram stamp and create personalized stationary for you or your pen-pals.

Do you love the special touch of a handwritten note? Do you have pen-pals who celebrates paper-craft? Create and send memorable stationary made with your personal monogram.

We will explore:

  • Printmaking techniques
  • Patterns

We will make:

  • A personal, monogram stamp (unmounted) (~ 2 ¼ in. x 2 ¼ in.)
  • A set of ten (10) personalized monogram stationary and matching baronial envelopes. (A6 Cards - 6 ¼ in. x 4 ⅝ in. folded)

Materials You Keep ($25 - included in total price):

  • Stamp carving tool (Speedball No.1 Lino Cutter Assortment with five cutters.)
  • Carving block
  • Stonehenge Pure Cotton Paper

✔Before the Workshop:

  • Choose a letter you would like to carve into a stamp. We’ve put together a document with some great numbered samples. Please send us an email with your letter selection (e.g. A1, J6, etc.) Feel free to find or create your own letter as well. Keep the letter size with a 2 ¼ in. x 2 ¼ in. square to properly fit your carving block and card.
