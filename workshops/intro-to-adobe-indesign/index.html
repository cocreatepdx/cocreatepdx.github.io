Learn the fundamentals of Adobe InDesign

Are you:

  • the person in your office responsible for creating posters, flyers, and other advertising
  • a writer and self-publisher who needs to typeset your work and automate page numbers, section markers, and table of contents
  • a small business owner or entrepreneur who needs to create professional documents and marketing materials
  • a current (or just beginning) digital scrapbooker who wants full creative freedom to build your pages
  • looking to add Adobe InDesign to your list of marketable skills

This three (3) part series of workshops will be an introduction to the desktop publishing application Adobe InDesign.

You do need access to Adobe Illustrator. You can download a free trial here

We will meet Mondays from 6pm to 8pm. Interested, but this day/time does not work for you? Please tell us your availability.

Satisfaction Guarantee: We are confident that you will be pleased with the Intro to Adobe InDesign Series; however, if at the end you are not completely satisfied, we offer you a nearly-full refund of $114 (less 5% processing fee).

You will learn:

Part 1 – Introduction to the InDesign

Monday, July 11, 2016  @  6pm - 8pm

  • InDesign general interface: workspaces, panels, tools, etc.
  • Document setup, margin, bleeds, pages, layers
  • Placing images and document links
  • Adding and manipulating text
  • Character and paragraph settings

Part 2 – more

Monday, July 18, 2016  @  6pm - 8pm

  • Character and paragraph styles
  • Bullets and numbering
  • Master pages, page numbering, section markers
  • Threading text
  • Grids, columns, and gutters

Part 3 – Getting Setup: Domain, Hosting, FTP, and Personalized Email

Monday, July 25, 2016  @  6pm - 8pm

  • Data merge
  • Hyperlinks
  • Generating a table of contents
