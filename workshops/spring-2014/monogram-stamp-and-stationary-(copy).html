Monogram Stamp & Stationary
Monday 3/3/2014
1pm – 3pm
NE Portland
We had a lovely time at the Monogram Stamp & Stationary workshop. Do you want to see this in workshop again? Request it!

Hand-carve a monogram stamp and create memorable, personalized stationary for family and friends.

Welcome to the Monogram Stamp & Stationary workshop. You will be hand-carving a stamp featuring the letter(s) of your choice and applying said stamp to build a personalized set of monogram stationary. This is a great project for yourself or as a gift for your pen-pal family and friends.

You leave with:

  • Set of Ten Personalized Monogram Stationary with Envelopes
  • Your Hand-carved Monogram Stamp
  • Carving tools
  • Stamp Resource Guide (Digital) with guality links to continue exploring stamp making
Cost of Materials: $25 Requirements: None
